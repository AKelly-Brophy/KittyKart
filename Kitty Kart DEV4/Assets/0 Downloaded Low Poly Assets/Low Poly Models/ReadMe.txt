LOW POLY MODELS is a pack of polygonal-stylized models optimized for both PC and mobile platforms.

This pack currently contains 314 models (including variations):
-Cars
-Environment objects
-Foodstuffs
-Furnitures
-Windmills and wind turbines
...and much more!

Some models have separate component meshes, such as car wheels and windmill rotors.

All meshes use standard materials.
Because of user preferences, colliders are not included.
You can, of course, easily add mesh colliders to the models you need.

Package will always be updated to include more!
